You, legendary warrior, have just killed King Thales. You have saved the peninsula from his tyranny. King Solon welcomes you back from your journey, though his brown hair now turned grey, and his face much more wrinkled than before. Despite this, he still has a warm smile on his face. Finally, peace has now returned to the land. All of the bards of the land now sing of the courageous hero who put an end to Thales' control over the peninsula.
